# VISION — what this system is ultimately for (operator, 2026-07-14)

Recorded from the operator's dictated statement; every holistic review measures the setup against
this description: what matches, what doesn't, what is missing.

## The model

A combination of an **AI engineering manager and AI engineers**. Claude and Codex are both
individual contributors (architects, highly skilled executors) AND management capacity:

- **Claude / the orchestrator = the AI engineering manager** — orchestrates, judges, reviews,
  synthesizes, holds the trust boundary.
- **Codex = both manager and executor** — plans, drafts, implements, researches.

## The end-to-end capability the operator wants

"Drop any idea onto you guys" and the system does everything:

1. **Grill the idea** — adversarial review, independent research by both models, falsification.
2. **Review / decide** — reconciled recommendation to the operator.
3. **Break it down into tickets** — decomposition to executable specs.
4. **Log tickets in a tracking system** (Jira / Linear).
5. **Iteratively improve the plan** — bounded review, revise, approve.
6. **Iteratively execute** — dispatch, checks, review, repair.
7. **Review results of work** — independent review, evidence-bound verdicts.
8. **Test it** — deterministic checks plus fixed test tasks.
9. **Set it up running** — deploy and operate.
10. **Maintain it** — ongoing operation, monitoring, upkeep of what it ships.
